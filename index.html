Nikolaos Tziavelis (Nikos)

I am a PhD candidate at Northeastern University and part of the DATA Lab. I am very lucky to be advised by Prof. Mirek Riedewald and Prof. Wolfgang Gatterbauer.

I work on problems that span both theoretical and practical aspects of data systems, with the goal of extending them with improved algorithms that achieve non-trivial guarantees. A lot of my recent work has focused on query optimization, and in particular, avoiding expensive many-to-many joins.

My research is generously supported by a Google PhD fellowship (Structured Data and Database Management area).

    

I am in the academic job market this year! Get in touch if you have any opportunities to discuss.
