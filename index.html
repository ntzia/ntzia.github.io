Nikolaos Tziavelis (Nikos)

I am a 3rd year PhD student at Northeastern University and part of the DATA Lab. I am glad to be advised by Prof. Mirek Riedewald and Prof. Wolfgang Gatterbauer.

In my research, I try to find the sweet spot between the theory and practice of Computer Science with a focus on data management. I am currently working on the interplay between joins and ranking in databases.
